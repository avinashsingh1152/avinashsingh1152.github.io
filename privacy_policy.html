Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

Welcome to the Bhagavad Gita (Audio & Text) app. Your spiritual journey and privacy are of utmost importance to us. This policy explains how we handle information within our application.

Privacy Commitment: Our app is designed to be a "Zero Data Collection" service. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, usage data, or device identifiers.

1. Information Collection

We do not require any user registration, sign-up, or profile creation. As a result:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • We do not track your physical location.
  • We do not access your contacts or files.

2. Local Storage (Device Only)

To provide features like "Bookmarking" or "Last Read Verse," the app may store small amounts of data locally on your device's internal storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is not accessible by us or any third party.
  • Is deleted automatically if you uninstall the app.

3. Audio and Media

The audio recitations of the Bhagavad Gita are either streamed or cached locally for your convenience. No data regarding your listening habits or preferences is transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app is built using industry-standard tools. While we do not collect data, some basic technical information may be processed by Google Play Services for app stability and updates. You can view their policy at Google Privacy & Terms.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is a safe, educational, and spiritual tool for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to stay compliant with global standards. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
